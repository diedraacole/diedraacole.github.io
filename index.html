Diedra Cole: A Bio

Hi, I'm Diedra, but you can call me Dee!

I'm a GIT undergrad at ASU Online studying Web Design and Development. My passion for web design started a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away when I learned basic HTML via Google to make myself and my friends super cool Myspace profiles.
Real original, eh?

Years later, I decided, "hey, that was a lot of fun—let's do that for a living!", and here I am, in the beginning of my senior year, taking as many courses and coding bootcamps as time allows to become the best designer I can be!

Educational & Professional Goals

When it comes to the web, I'm a sucker for good design, but something that really warms my heart and is extremely important to me is good UX design. I hope to become a strong, well-rounded web designer and developer that advocates for inclusive, accessible, and clean design for all users!

Personal Life

When I'm not slinging coffee at Starbucks, you can most likely find me falling down the rabbit hole of my latest obsession whether that's binging a new video game or tv series, reading a Sci-Fi/Fantasy book (like one of the many Stephen King novels I thrifted from Goodwill but haven't read yet), learning to play a new song on my keyboard until my arms give out, or buying too many houseplants (thanks quarantine).

When I'm not being a complete hermit, I enjoy traveling to visit friends that live out-of-state as well as traveling to see my favorite bands live. I also take part in recording a D&D podcast that I co-host with my husband and four of our friends. Recently, I have been learning new coding languages with my husband as a way of challenging ourselves and spending time together whilst also pursuing our educational and professional goals.
